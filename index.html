Services

I will NOT draw any of the following:
~ PNSFW
~ Excessive blood/gore
~ Anything that I feel is offensive
I have the right to refuse to accept any commission for any reason
I am allowed to post the piece to my social media
Reselling my artwork or claiming it as your own work is not allowed
Upon request, I will update you throughout the progress of the piece as such:
~ Finished sketch
~ Partial color
During each of these phases, I will present you with your commission WIP and you can let me know if anything should be changed. I will not accept excessive/ drastic changes
Emotes can take anywhere from 1-3 days
Character Illustrations can take anywhere from 3-7 days depending on what you ask for
